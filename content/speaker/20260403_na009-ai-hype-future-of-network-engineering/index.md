Nine network automation engineers convene for a roundtable examining AI's practical effects on the field. Topics include how AI functions as a productivity multiplier rather than a replacement technology, the shift from code generation to verification responsibilities, workplace pressure from AI mandates, concerns about skill atrophy, risks of unmaintained AI-generated code, and balancing development velocity against quality standards.
