[NLNAM](https://net-auto.nl/) is the NL Network Automation Meetup — a community of network automation enthusiasts of all levels and backgrounds, coming together to share knowledge, experiences, and a good conversation.

I founded and organise NLNAM with the belief that the best way to grow as a community is by sharing openly: what works, what doesn't, and what we're still figuring out. Whether you're just getting started with automation or have been doing it for years, there's always something to learn and something to share.

We meet approximately every three months at different locations across the Netherlands. Each event features talks from practitioners in the field, followed by plenty of time for discussion and networking.

**Interested in joining or speaking?** Find all information at [net-auto.nl](https://net-auto.nl/).
